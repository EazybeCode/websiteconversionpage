Getting Started

Install the Eazybe Chrome Extension from the Chrome Web Store, open WhatsApp Web, click the integration icon, select HubSpot, and login with your HubSpot credentials. That's it! Your conversations will start syncing automatically.

You need: (1) Google Chrome browser, (2) A HubSpot account (works with Free, Starter, and Professional plans), (3) WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business on your phone. No API access or technical setup required.

With the Teams plan, you can add team members from the Eazybe dashboard. Each member installs the Chrome extension and connects their WhatsApp. All conversations are then visible in the shared Team Inbox.
